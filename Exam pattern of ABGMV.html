Exam pattern of ABGMV

The Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal (ABGMV) conducts examinations for Indian classical music and dance.
The exam pattern for ABGMV exams consists of both theory and practical components.

Theory Exam

The theory exam is conducted online for exams up to Visharad level (Prarambhik to Visharad).
The Alankar exam theory exam is conducted online only in the November session.
Students are required to have a computer/laptop, strong internet connection, scanner, printer, mobile phone with Zoom app to take the online theory exam.

Practical Exam

Practical exams are conducted in person at affiliated exam centers across India and abroad.
The format of the practical exam varies depending on the level and subject.
You can find more information about the ABGMV exam pattern, including the syllabus and marking scheme, on the ABGMV website click here
